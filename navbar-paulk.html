About us

The SLP-SL was established in January 2018 and registered with the Corporate Affairs and Freetown City Council as a Community Based organisation (CBO) within Freetown - Sierra Leone. The organization has a membership of Ten (10) as founding members with openness to accommodate many willing others desiring to be part of us.


Who we are

Supports for Less Privilege Sierra Leone (SLPSL is a both children, youth and adult serving agency that seeks to advocates and lobby for the rights and empowerment of children, youth and women, Environment and promote democracy, good governance and national accountability and transparency. It is a Community non-governmental organization that is non-profit making, non-partisan non-tribal but independently impartial. It was established in January 2018 and we are currently doing our registration with the Corporate Affairs in Sierra Leone.


Our mission

Facilitate processes of justice and inclusion of marginalized and deprived children, widows and amputees into community development agendas. Our Core Values. Transparency: We strive to build a culture of transparency and we demand others to be transparent too in all our interactions and networking. Accountability: We ensure and affirm accountability in all our activities to build a better and prosperous Sierra Leone. Respect: We respect the dignity of every human person including children and all persons we work for and within all our interventions.


Aim and objectives

Provide educational assistant in a form of scholarship to less privileged children in the targeted communities. To increase access to affordable and quality education for the less privilege children across the targeted areas. Enhanced capacities of 250 widows in livelihood skills and enterprise development in these communities through microfinance support and bussiness training.
